### About

A simple game I made for my Grade 10 ICS Summative.

### Gameplay

Controls:

- left/right = walk
- up = jump (up again to double jump)
- down = fall faster
- z = shoot
- x = stab/dash
- c = swing
- v = glide (only when falling)
- enter = select (in menu)
